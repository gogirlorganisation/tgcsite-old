Workshops

We plan to organise monthly workshops, held at a school over two weeks.The workshop will start off with some form of public speaking, fun group acting improvisations and games on Day 1. Through such activities, we aim to make the students feel comfortable and confident in their skin and to inspire the ability to work in a group, whilst keeping things light and fun. The students will also get the opportunity to personally interact with their respective Mentors.On Day 2, we’ll have a keynote speaker — an inspirational woman in the field of programming. We will also focus on the programming and mathematical aspect on Day 2, with trivia quizzes, conceptual lectures and acquaint them with our platform. Workshops will use school equipment — providing resources to students without them, which is salient for underprivileged government school students. Through our workshops, we will aim to instill an inherent sense of confidence, compatibility and capability in our students.
