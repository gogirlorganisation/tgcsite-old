Oakridge International School

August 2018

The workshop held at Oakridge International School (Newton Campus) took place over eleven days between July 23rd and August 14th. A total of thirty-seven students attended the workshop. The workshop left a sizeable impact on many of the girls, some of whom, after some more training in programming, intend to join our team at The Girl Code as mentors to help conduct workshops.
