The Shri Ram School, Moulsari

December 2018

The Girl Code’s latest workshop was held at The Shri Ram School Moulsari and was sponsored by Litas. The participants were challenged and kept on their toes with various problems, and were able to quickly grasp information without getting overwhelmed. The workshop harbored an engaging and dynamic atmosphere and encouraged lots of participation.
