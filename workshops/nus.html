NUS Workshop

August 2018

The Girl Code conducted an interactive workshop at the National University of Singapore in August 2018. Read below for more details.

The workshop, held at the NUS School of Computing in Singapore, was six hours long (from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m.) and hosted over sixty-five students from more than ten schools. A large number of students especially attended from National Junior College and GIIS Punggol.
